Welcome to SSUET Canada Alumni association's official web portal. Established in 2015, SSUET Canada Alumni association was developed as a way to connect individuals hailing from the reputable institution, residing in Canada.
It all started in 2010, when Muzammil Mukhtar 97-CE arranged a meeting gathering a group SSUET Graduates to discuss the idea of creating a proper Alumni organization for SSUETIANS in Canada and North America. The idea turned into reality when Faraz Farooqui 97-CE created the yahoo email group in 2010 & later ZeeShan Anwar 97-CE initiated the Whatsapp group in 2015. 142 members later, SSUET.CA/Engineers now is an ever-growing platform that provides networking, socializing and professional growth opportunities to SSUETIANS living in Canada.

If you are a SSUETIAN, join us on board. Browse our website for related information, refresh your memories, find some old friends and make some new ones. Let's join hands to help each other grow and connect.
